IP Addresses:

IP addresses associated with any comment edits are logged.

This includes rating positive/neutral/negative, tagging, marking flagged or deleted or editing the comment text.
Incorrect password attempts also have their associated IP's logged.
This does not include editing your comments for one of your students.
Entries on the contact form do not have their IP addresses logged.

Your Comments:

I do not save any finished comments, student names, class names or patterns.

The save and load file is done 100% in the browser. The server never sees those files.

Cookies:

Cookies are not required to access Comment Breeze and no functionality will change if you disable them.

Comment Breeze does not set or use cookies, but Google Analytics does.

Email addresses:

Email addresses or names submitted on the contact form will never be shared or sold.
